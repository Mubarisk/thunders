About Us

Thunders is an arts and sports club located in perunthallloor .The club started with only five boys in 2013.At the beginning of the club we named silver star arts and sports club but after 6 years(2019) we were renamed by thunders.Now the thunders is filled with 35+ people .Many people help to improve the club by giving money or giving better advice.now we have a good committee to improve the club .mr junaidh is the secretary of th club, mr shuhaib is the president and mr mubaris is the treasurer of the club.The club is mainly focus on sports and social work.we achieve many momentos in sports

We thank everyone who help us to create the club and running it successfully THANK YOU

Our Works

Sports

The Thunders club is given a big importance in sports .Many of the thunders members have good potential in sports.Thunders have two football team senior and junior ,they achieve many trophies in the football matches.

Social work

The Thunders club gives good importance to society.We keep clean our village ,helps people who need help with work or money.we are always ready to help the society any way , the members of the thunders are very happy to work for the society.

Arts

We hase many artists in our club ,but badly they are not ready to express their work to out side.but in future we hope the good artists will come in our club.
